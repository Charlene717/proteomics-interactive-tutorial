📌 教學註記與細節

下方為閱讀教學 HTML 與本 reference 比對後，發現的可能不完整、易誤解或可加強之處。不修改原教學檔案，僅在此說明以利參照。

The notes below collect potentially incomplete, misleading or strengthen-worthy items discovered when cross-checking the tutorial HTML against the proteomics literature. The original tutorial files are left untouched; explanations are appended here for cross-reference.

1. MaxLFQ「大多數蛋白不變」假設下才有效 · MaxLFQ assumes most proteins are unchanged

教學在 Quantification 章節推薦 MaxLFQ 為 LFQ 主流，但需指出其內隱假設：原論文 Cox et al. Mol Cell Proteomics 13:2513, 2014（doi:10.1074/mcp.M113.031591）以 peptide ratio 中位數 ≈ 1 為前提，等同假設「絕大多數蛋白未變」。當研究設計屬全域擾動（例如全面 phospho-treatment、IP 富集、knockout 對 wild-type 含有大量缺失蛋白）時，median 中心化會誤判方向；建議改採 (i) spike-in 對照（UPS2、Pierce digest）；(ii) loess / VSN（Huber 2002）區段標準化；或 (iii) 在 limma / proDA / msqrob2（Goeminne 2020）中明確用 housekeeping 蛋白做 reference。iBAQ 與 Top3 雖較粗糙但不依賴此假設，可作為交叉驗證。

The Quantification chapter recommends MaxLFQ as the default for LFQ, but it carries a strong implicit assumption: the original Cox et al. paper (Mol Cell Proteomics 13:2513, 2014, doi:10.1074/mcp.M113.031591) requires the median peptide ratio to be ≈ 1, i.e. that the bulk of proteins are unchanged. Whole-proteome perturbations (global phospho-treatment, IP enrichment, knockouts with widespread protein loss) violate this and bias the direction of fold-changes after median-centering. Cross-check with (i) spike-in standards (UPS2, Pierce digest); (ii) loess or VSN normalisation (Huber 2002); or (iii) housekeeping-anchored reference normalisation inside limma / proDA / msqrob2 (Goeminne 2020). iBAQ and Top3 are coarser but do not rely on this assumption and serve as orthogonal sanity checks.

2. TMT ratio compression：必加 IRS 或 SPS-MS3 · TMT ratio compression and the need for IRS / SPS-MS3

教學介紹 TMT/TMTpro 為高 multiplex 定量首選，但若僅做 MS2 報導離子定量，co-isolation interference 會造成嚴重的 ratio compression（觀測 fold-change 被壓縮至真值 30–60%）。McAlister et al. Anal Chem 86:7150, 2014（doi:10.1021/ac502040v）的 SPS-MS3（MultiNotch）顯著降低此效應，但犧牲掃描速度；Plubell et al. Mol Cell Proteomics 16:873, 2017（doi:10.1074/mcp.M116.065524）提出 Internal Reference Scaling (IRS) 處理跨 TMT plex（>16 樣本）的 batch effect，已成為 TMT 多 batch 研究 SOP。教學若僅示範單一 TMT plex，請補上：(i) MS2 vs MS3 取捨；(ii) IRS / common reference channel 設計；(iii) Phillips 2024（PXD040000）等 benchmark 顯示 SPS-MS3 + IRS 才能達到 LFQ 水準的精準度。

TMT / TMTpro is presented as the go-to high-multiplex strategy, but pure MS2-level reporter quantification suffers severe ratio compression from co-isolation interference (observed fold-changes shrink to 30–60% of truth). McAlister et al. (Anal Chem 86:7150, 2014, doi:10.1021/ac502040v) introduced SPS-MS3 (MultiNotch) to mitigate this at the cost of scan rate, and Plubell et al. (Mol Cell Proteomics 16:873, 2017, doi:10.1074/mcp.M116.065524) developed Internal Reference Scaling (IRS) to remove cross-plex batch effects when stacking >16 samples — now the de facto SOP for multi-plex TMT designs. If the tutorial shows only a single plex, add a panel on (i) MS2 vs MS3 trade-offs; (ii) IRS / common-reference-channel design; and (iii) Phillips 2024 benchmarks showing SPS-MS3 + IRS reaches LFQ-level accuracy.

3. DIA vs DDA 動態範圍與隨機性差異 · DIA vs DDA: dynamic range and stochastic sampling

教學在 LC-MS/MS Acquisition 章節並陳 DDA 與 DIA，但未指出兩者最關鍵差異：DDA 為 stochastic sampling（top-N 選擇前體），同一樣本重複跑會得到不同 missing pattern；DIA / SWATH（Gillet et al. Mol Cell Proteomics 11:O111.016717, 2012，doi:10.1074/mcp.O111.016717）以等寬 m/z 視窗連續掃描，提供 deterministic、可重現 的 MS2，更適合大規模 cohort。但 DIA 對 chromatographic alignment 與 fragment-level deconvolution（DIA-NN, Demichev 2020；Spectronaut；EncyclopeDIA, Searle 2018）要求極高，且 library-free direct-DIA 在低豐度區域仍劣於 spectral library 模式約 10–20% protein ID。教學若推 DIA，請補上 fragment-ion interference 與 retention-time alignment 的章節。

The acquisition chapter contrasts DDA and DIA but understates the most consequential difference: DDA is stochastic (top-N precursor selection), so replicate injections produce different missing-value patterns, while DIA / SWATH (Gillet et al. Mol Cell Proteomics 11:O111.016717, 2012, doi:10.1074/mcp.O111.016717) scans fixed m/z windows and is deterministic and reproducible, which is why it dominates large cohorts. The trade-off is that DIA needs strong chromatographic alignment and fragment-level deconvolution (DIA-NN, Demichev 2020; Spectronaut; EncyclopeDIA, Searle 2018), and library-free direct-DIA still loses ~10–20% of low-abundance IDs versus spectral-library mode. Add a panel on fragment-ion interference and RT alignment if DIA is recommended.

4. Top3 vs iBAQ vs MaxLFQ 半定量比較 · Top3 vs iBAQ vs MaxLFQ semi-quant comparison

教學介紹 absolute quantification 時常將 Top3、iBAQ、MaxLFQ 並列，但三者推導動機不同，混用易導致誤讀：(i) Top3（Silva et al. Mol Cell Proteomics 5:144, 2006，doi:10.1074/mcp.M500230-MCP200）為 MSE 時期 average top-3 peptide intensity；(ii) iBAQ（Schwanhäusser et al. Nature 473:337, 2011，doi:10.1038/nature10098）為 sum-intensity ÷ theoretical-tryptic-peptide-count；(iii) MaxLFQ 為 pairwise peptide-ratio 最大概似解。建議在教學補上 Ahrné et al. Proteomics 13:2567, 2013（doi:10.1002/pmic.201300135）的 benchmark，顯示 MaxLFQ 在相對定量精準度最高，但 iBAQ 用於跨蛋白絕對含量比較更穩定；Top3 在 SWATH/DIA library 才推薦。

Absolute-quantification discussions often list Top3, iBAQ and MaxLFQ side-by-side, but their motivations differ and mixing them invites misreading: (i) Top3 (Silva et al. Mol Cell Proteomics 5:144, 2006, doi:10.1074/mcp.M500230-MCP200) is the average of the three most-intense peptides from the MSE era; (ii) iBAQ (Schwanhäusser et al. Nature 473:337, 2011, doi:10.1038/nature10098) divides summed intensity by the number of theoretical tryptic peptides; (iii) MaxLFQ is the maximum-likelihood pairwise peptide-ratio solution. Reference Ahrné et al. (Proteomics 13:2567, 2013, doi:10.1002/pmic.201300135): MaxLFQ wins on relative quant precision, iBAQ wins on cross-protein absolute abundance estimates, and Top3 is mostly a DIA / SWATH library construct now.

5. Phospho-enrichment 偏好與 normalization 必要性 · Phospho enrichment bias and the need for matched normalisation

教學在 PTM / phosphoproteomics 章節介紹 IMAC / TiO₂ 富集為標準流程，但需指出富集存在系統性偏好：(i) IMAC 偏好 multiply-phosphorylated peptides；(ii) TiO₂ 對 mono-phospho 有更好的 yield；(iii) 不同 batch 富集效率變化可達 2–3 倍，必須以 matched total-proteome run 作標準化才能區分 phospho-fold-change 與 protein-level fold-change。Humphrey et al. Nat Protoc 13:1897, 2018（doi:10.1038/s41596-018-0014-9，EasyPhos）詳細列出 SOP；Krug et al. Mol Cell Proteomics 18:576, 2019（doi:10.1074/mcp.RA118.001230）的「regress out protein-level change」是 CPTAC 標準做法。教學若僅示範 phospho intensities，請補上 total-proteome regression 與 site-level FDR。

Phosphoproteomics in the PTM chapter introduces IMAC / TiO₂ enrichment as standard, but enrichment carries systematic biases: (i) IMAC favours multiply-phosphorylated peptides; (ii) TiO₂ has higher yield for mono-phosphopeptides; (iii) batch-to-batch enrichment efficiency varies 2–3-fold, so a matched total-proteome run is required to separate phospho fold-change from protein-level fold-change. Humphrey et al. (Nat Protoc 13:1897, 2018, doi:10.1038/s41596-018-0014-9, EasyPhos) gives the practical SOP, and Krug et al. (Mol Cell Proteomics 18:576, 2019, doi:10.1074/mcp.RA118.001230) defines the CPTAC convention of regressing out matched protein-level changes. Add a panel on total-proteome regression and site-level FDR if only phospho intensities are shown.

6. 抗體驗證五支柱與 Affinity 章節補強 · Antibody validation: the five Pillars

教學 Affinity 章節介紹 IP-MS / ChIP / Western 等使用抗體，但未具體列出抗體驗證標準。Uhlén et al. Nat Methods 13:823, 2016（doi:10.1038/nmeth.3995）提出 5 Pillars：(1) 遺傳策略（KO / KD）；(2) 正交方法（mass spec / RNA-seq 對比）；(3) 獨立抗體（兩個獨立 epitope）；(4) 標籤蛋白表達；(5) 免疫捕捉 + MS 驗證。YCharOS（ycharos.com，Biass et al. Nat Protoc，2024）以 KO 對照系統測試抗體性能；Antibodypedia 與 Human Protein Atlas 提供 antibody scoring。教學若推任何抗體應用，建議補上至少 Pillar 1+2 為 minimum bar。

The Affinity chapter covers IP-MS / ChIP / Western using antibodies, but does not specify validation standards. Uhlén et al. (Nat Methods 13:823, 2016, doi:10.1038/nmeth.3995) defines the 5 Pillars: (1) genetic strategy (KO / KD); (2) orthogonal methods (mass spec / RNA-seq); (3) independent antibodies against distinct epitopes; (4) tagged-protein expression; (5) immuno-capture + MS verification. YCharOS (ycharos.com; Biass et al., Nat Protoc, 2024) tests commercial antibodies in KO cell lines, and Antibodypedia / Human Protein Atlas provide antibody scoring. Any antibody application in the tutorial should at least clear Pillars 1 and 2.

7. PTM site localization：Mascot Delta vs ptmRS vs PhosphoRS · PTM site localisation scores

教學在 PTM 章節常以「peptide 被鑑定 = site 也被鑑定」隱含對應，但對於含有多個 S/T/Y 的 phosphopeptide，site localization 機率 必須額外評估。Mascot Delta（Savitski et al. 2011）為早期方法，需 Mascot search engine；ptmRS / PhosphoRS（Taus et al. J Proteome Res 10:5354, 2011，doi:10.1021/pr200611n）以 binomial probability 計算 site-level confidence，現整合於 Proteome Discoverer；Ascore（Beausoleil et al. Nat Biotechnol 24:1285, 2006，doi:10.1038/nbt1240）為 MaxQuant 預設；LuciPHOr2（Fermin et al. 2015）為 open-source 替代。建議教學標明「peptide FDR ≠ site FDR」，並使用 site-localization probability > 0.75（class I site, Olsen 2006 標準）作為下游分析的 cutoff。

The PTM chapter often implies "peptide identified = site identified", but for phosphopeptides containing multiple S/T/Y residues a separate site-localisation probability is required. Mascot Delta (Savitski 2011) was the early scheme; ptmRS / PhosphoRS (Taus et al., J Proteome Res 10:5354, 2011, doi:10.1021/pr200611n) computes site-level confidence by a binomial model and is now integrated into Proteome Discoverer; Ascore (Beausoleil et al., Nat Biotechnol 24:1285, 2006, doi:10.1038/nbt1240) is the MaxQuant default; LuciPHOr2 (Fermin et al. 2015) is an open-source alternative. Make clear that "peptide FDR ≠ site FDR", and use site-localisation probability > 0.75 (the Olsen 2006 Class I cut-off) for downstream interpretation.

8. 蛋白質推斷與 razor peptide 風險 · Protein inference and the razor-peptide problem

教學在 Identification / Search 章節介紹 protein FDR，但需補上 protein inference 與 razor / shared peptide 處理：Nesvizhskii & Aebersold Mol Cell Proteomics 4:1419, 2005（doi:10.1074/mcp.R500012-MCP200）說明 ProteinProphet / MaxQuant razor 策略；The & MacCoss J Proteome Res 15:1190, 2016（doi:10.1021/acs.jproteome.5b01019，picked target-decoy）指出 group-FDR 與 protein-level decoy 設計差異。對於含大量 isoform / paralog 的研究（如 keratin、HLA、Ig），razor peptide 會被任意指派給組內第一個蛋白，導致 isoform 級定量無意義；建議改用 protein group 或 picked group 程序，並回報「unique peptides ≥ 2」與 percolator / mokapot 計算的 PSM-level q-value。

The Identification / Search chapter covers protein FDR but omits protein inference and the razor / shared peptide problem. Nesvizhskii & Aebersold (Mol Cell Proteomics 4:1419, 2005, doi:10.1074/mcp.R500012-MCP200) describes ProteinProphet and the MaxQuant razor strategy; The & MacCoss (J Proteome Res 15:1190, 2016, doi:10.1021/acs.jproteome.5b01019, picked target-decoy) explains why group-FDR with picked decoys differs from naive protein-level FDR. For isoform / paralog–heavy studies (keratin, HLA, Ig) razor peptides get arbitrarily assigned to the first protein in the group, making isoform-resolved quantitation meaningless. Use protein-group or picked-group inference, require "unique peptides ≥ 2", and report PSM-level q-values from Percolator or mokapot.
