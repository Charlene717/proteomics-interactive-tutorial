STEP 5 / 9

品質管控 (QC)

從層析峰形到 PSM/peptide/protein 計數、CV、contaminants 與 batch effect——多層次 QC 是統計分析可信的前提。

From chromatographic peaks to PSM/peptide/protein counts, CV, contaminants, and batch effects — multi-layer QC is the prerequisite for trustworthy statistics.

概覽

QC 的三個層次

蛋白質體學的 QC 必須跨越三個層次：(1) 儀器層次——層析峰寬、ESI 噴霧穩定性、質量精度；(2) 樣品層次——PSM/peptide/protein 計數、CV、missing rate、contaminants %；(3) 群體層次——batch effect、PCA 結構、樣品聚類是否符合預期。

跳過 QC 是新手最常犯的錯誤。一個 batch effect 失控的資料集，會讓所有下游分析（DE、enrichment）失去意義。

Proteomics QC spans three layers: (1) Instrument — chromatographic peak width, ESI spray stability, mass accuracy; (2) Sample — PSM/peptide/protein counts, CV, missing rate, contaminants %; (3) Cohort — batch effects, PCA structure, expected sample clustering.

Skipping QC is the most common newbie mistake. A dataset with uncontrolled batch effects renders all downstream analyses (DE, enrichment) meaningless.

儀器 QC

一、儀器層次 QC：每天/每週檢查

🎯 質量精度

Orbitrap 應 ≤ 5 ppm 偏差。每次 run 後抽 50 個高分 PSM 計算 median error。長期漂移可由 lockmass 或 calibration 修正。

Orbitrap should keep ≤ 5 ppm. Pull 50 top PSMs after each run; compute median error. Long-term drift can be corrected by lockmass or calibration.

📊 峰寬 (FWHM)

90 min 梯度典型 FWHM 約 18–35 秒。突然變寬代表柱子老化或樣品堆積；變窄可能流速異常。

For 90-min gradients, typical FWHM is 18–35 s. Sudden widening = column aging or sample carry-over; narrowing = flow issue.

💨 ESI 噴霧穩定性

TIC (Total Ion Current) chromatogram 應為「滑順丘形」。突然 dropout（dip）暗示 spray 不穩，需檢查 emitter / 接頭。

The TIC chromatogram should be a smooth hump. Sudden dropouts indicate unstable spray — check emitter and connections.

🔄 RT 穩定性

同樣品的內標胜肽 RT 應 ≤ 30 秒漂移。系統性漂移可在 search 時設更大 RT tolerance；隨機漂移多為層析問題。

RT of internal-standard peptides should drift ≤ 30 s across runs. Systematic drift can be tolerated in search; random drift = chromatography issue.

🧂 碎裂效率

HCD NCE 過低 → b/y 不足；過高 → 內部碎片過多。理想 b/y 共佔 MS2 強度 70% 以上。

HCD NCE too low → too few b/y ions; too high → too many internal fragments. Ideally b/y account for >70% of MS2 intensity.

📍 用 HeLa QC sample

每天/每批跑一次 HeLa 標準（如 Pierce HeLa Digest）。長期追蹤 # protein、# peptide、% MT、retention 等指標可早期發現儀器衰退。

Run a HeLa standard (e.g., Pierce HeLa Digest) daily/per-batch. Long-term tracking of # protein / # peptide / % MT / RT detects instrument degradation early.

互動模擬

批次效應 PCA 模擬

觀察樣品在 PCA 中是按生物條件（Treatment vs Control）分群，還是按批次（Batch 1 vs Batch 2）分群。若 batch 主導，則需要 ComBat、limma::removeBatchEffect 或 randomized design 處理。

Do samples cluster by biological condition (Treatment vs Control) or by batch (Batch 1 vs 2)? If batch dominates, apply ComBat, limma::removeBatchEffect, or randomized design.

⚠️
專家建議：① 在 QC 階段就移除 Contaminants 與 Reverse hits，否則會污染下游 DE。② 永遠先看 PCA，再決定是否要 batch correction。③ 不要把所有樣品打散後集中分析；應先做 per-batch QC。
Expert tips: ① Remove contaminants & reverse hits at the QC stage — else they corrupt DE. ② Always run PCA before deciding on batch correction. ③ Don't immediately pool all samples — do per-batch QC first.

1. PC1 separates batches rather than biological conditions. Best response?

A. 忽略，直接做 DEA. Ignore and proceed with DE
B. 用 ComBat 或 limma::removeBatchEffect 進行 batch correction，並在 design matrix 中加入 batch 變數B. Apply ComBat or limma::removeBatchEffect, and add batch as a covariate in the design matrix
C. 重做實驗C. Redo the experiment
D. 將所有樣品平均成單一值D. Average all samples into one

2. Keratins account for 18% of total intensity. Most likely cause?

A. 樣品真的富含 keratinA. Sample is genuinely keratin-rich
B. 質譜儀故障B. MS malfunction

D. Trypsin 純度不夠D. Trypsin purity issue

3. 為何 DIA 的 missing rate 通常遠低於 DDA？

3. Why does DIA generally have a much lower missing rate than DDA?

A. DIA 無差別碎裂所有窗內離子，不存在 DDA 的「Top-N 隨機選擇」缺失A. DIA fragments everything within windows — no "Top-N stochastic selection" gaps
B. DIA 使用更高解析度B. DIA uses higher resolution
C. DIA 自動填補缺失C. DIA auto-imputes missing values
D. DIA 樣品較濃D. DIA samples are more concentrated
