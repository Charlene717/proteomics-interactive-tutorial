轉譯後修飾 (PTM Analysis)

磷酸化、乙醯化、糖基化、泛素化——蛋白質功能調控的「動態語言」與富集策略。

Phospho-, acetyl-, glyco-, ubiquitin- — the "dynamic language" of protein regulation and the enrichment strategies behind it.

概覽

為什麼 PTM 是蛋白質功能的核心？

同一條蛋白序列在不同生理狀態下可能存在數十種不同的化學修飾形式（proteoforms）。一個磷酸化位點開或關，可能決定酵素活性、訊號傳遞、亞細胞定位、甚至細胞命運。

關鍵挑戰：PTM 胜肽的豐度通常比 unmodified peptide 低 1000–10000 倍，必須先用「富集 (enrichment)」把它們從複雜的胜肽混合物中拉出來，才能在 MS 上有效偵測。

A single protein sequence can exist in dozens of chemical forms (proteoforms) depending on physiological state. A single phosphosite toggle may decide enzyme activity, signaling, localization — even cell fate.

Key challenge: modified peptides are typically 1000–10000× less abundant than their unmodified counterparts. Without enrichment, MS can't see them in a complex mixture.

主要 PTM 類型

一、五大主要 PTM 與其分析

⚡ 磷酸化 (Phosphorylation)

S/T/Y 上 +80 Da。佔最大宗（人類預估 > 200k 位點）。
• 富集：TiO₂ MOAC、Fe-IMAC（Fe³⁺/Ti⁴⁺-NTA），磁珠或 spin tip 格式
• 儀器：HCD 為主，補 ETD 解析 PTM 位點
• 分析：MaxQuant + Andromeda；FragPipe + Localization；PhosphoSitePlus 註釋

+80 Da on S/T/Y. Most abundant (>200k human sites estimated).
• Enrichment: TiO₂ MOAC, Fe-IMAC (Fe³⁺/Ti⁴⁺-NTA) — magnetic beads or spin tips
• MS: HCD primary, ETD complements site localization
• Analysis: MaxQuant + Andromeda; FragPipe + PTM-Prophet; PhosphoSitePlus annotation

🌸 乙醯化 (Acetylation)

K 上 +42 Da。Histone 與代謝酵素的主要修飾。
• 富集：抗 acetyl-K 抗體 (PTMScan)；無 affinity 替代物。
• 注意：acetyl-K 與 trimethyl-K 質量近（差 0.036 Da），需高解析。
• 分析：MaxQuant variable mod + 抗體富集樣品；OpenSearch 可挖掘新位點。

+42 Da on K. Major modification on histones and metabolic enzymes.
• Enrichment: Anti-acetyl-K antibody (PTMScan) — no affinity surrogate.
• Note: acetyl-K and trimethyl-K differ by only 0.036 Da; high resolution required.
• Analysis: MaxQuant variable mod + antibody-enriched sample; OpenSearch finds novel sites.

🍬 糖基化 (Glycosylation)

N-glyco (NXS/T motif)、O-glyco (S/T)。質量高度異質，分析最具挑戰。
• 富集：HILIC、Lectin (ConA/WGA)、ZIC-HILIC、固相硼酸
• 儀器：必須 stepped HCD 或 EThcD
• 分析：Byonic、pGlyco3、MSFragger-Glyco

N-glyco (NXS/T motif) and O-glyco (S/T). Highly heterogeneous masses; most challenging.
• Enrichment: HILIC, lectin (ConA/WGA), ZIC-HILIC, solid-phase boronate
• MS: Stepped HCD or EThcD required
• Analysis: Byonic, pGlyco3, MSFragger-Glyco

🔗 泛素化 (Ubiquitination)

K 上連接 Ub。Trypsin 消化後，K 上殘留 GG (-Gly-Gly) 標記，質量 +114.04。
• 富集：抗 K-εGG 抗體 (PTMScan Ub)
• 限制：無法區分 Ub vs NEDD8 (兩者切後皆遺留 GG)
• 分析：MaxQuant；Open search 可發現 K-branching

Ubiquitin chains attached to K. Trypsin leaves a GG (-Gly-Gly) remnant (+114.04) on the modified K.
• Enrichment: Anti-K-εGG antibody (PTMScan Ub)
• Limit: Cannot distinguish Ub vs NEDD8 (both leave GG)
• Analysis: MaxQuant; open search reveals K-branching

📝 甲基化 (Methylation)

K 或 R 上 mono/di/tri-methyl。重要 histone & RNA-binding 蛋白修飾。
• 富集：無 universal affinity；用 SCX 或 SCX + IMAC 反向
• 分析：Open search，搭配 PTM 位點機率定位 (Ascore / ptmRS)

Mono/di/tri-methyl on K or R. Important on histones and RNA-binding proteins.
• Enrichment: No universal affinity; use SCX or SCX-reverse + IMAC
• Analysis: Open search + site localization probability (Ascore / ptmRS)

♻️ 其他重要 PTM

SUMOylation, palmitoylation, ADP-ribosylation, citrullination, hydroxylation, S-glutathionylation 等。每種有獨特化學策略——多數需專屬 antibody 或化學標籤。

SUMOylation, palmitoylation, ADP-ribosylation, citrullination, hydroxylation, S-glutathionylation, etc. Each has a unique chemistry — most need a dedicated antibody or chemical handle.

位點定位

三、PTM 位點定位 (Site Localization)

許多 PTM 胜肽包含多個可能位點（例如 S-T-Y 都可被磷酸化）。即使精確定位質量，MS2 譜中 diagnostic ions 才能區分哪個 specific 殘基被修飾。

主流評分系統：

  • Ascore (Beausoleil 2006) — 經典，二項統計
  • ptmRS (Taus 2011) — 整合於 Proteome Discoverer
  • PhosphoRS / PTMLocalizer — site probability output
  • Class I sites: localization probability > 0.75，delta score > 8 為高信心位點

Many PTM peptides contain multiple potential sites (e.g., S-T-Y can all be phosphorylated). Even with exact mass, only the MS2 diagnostic ions distinguish which residue is modified.

Major scoring systems:

  • Ascore (Beausoleil 2006) — classic binomial statistic
  • ptmRS (Taus 2011) — integrated in Proteome Discoverer
  • PhosphoRS / PTMLocalizer — site probability output
  • Class I sites: localization probability > 0.75 and delta score > 8 — high-confidence

[Group-specific Parameters]

  - Oxidation (M)
  - Acetyl (Protein N-term)
  - Phospho (STY)          # 關鍵

  - Carbamidomethyl (C)

[Global Parameters]
Min peptide length              = 7
Match between runs              = TRUE  (LFQ phospho 建議)
Re-quantify                     = TRUE
PSM FDR / Protein FDR           = 0.01 / 0.01
Site decoy FDR                  = 0.01

# 輸出: Phospho (STY)Sites.txt
# 重要欄位: Localization prob, Score diff, Multiplicity (1/2/3 phospho-site)
# FragPipe Phospho workflow
Workflow: LFQ-MBR (or TMT16-MS3) → enable "Phospho-STY"
MSFragger:
  variable_mod_01 = 79.96633 STY 3   # up to 3 phospho per peptide
  variable_mod_02 = 15.9949 M 3
  open_search     = false             # closed for known PTM

PTM-Prophet:
  enable           = true
  STY localization = true
  Threshold        = 0.75              # Class I site

Output:
  - psm.tsv             : PSM-level
  - peptide.tsv         : peptide
  - protein.tsv         : protein
  - Phospho_*.tsv       : site-level localization scores

📝 自我檢測

1. 你的磷酸化富集樣品中 phospho 佔比只有 25%，最可能的問題？

1. Phospho fraction is only 25% after enrichment. Likely problem?

A. 蛋白質太多A. Too many proteins
B. 富集條件 sub-optimal（loading buffer、binding time、bead 比例失調）B. Sub-optimal enrichment conditions (loading buffer, binding time, bead ratio)
C. 質譜儀解析度不夠C. Insufficient MS resolution
D. Trypsin 太活躍D. Trypsin too active

2. PTM 位點 localization probability = 0.5 代表？

2. PTM localization probability = 0.5 means?

A. 高信心位點A. High-confidence site
B. 不存在 PTMB. No PTM present
C. 位點不確定（兩個可能位置難以區分），不應作為 Class I 報告C. Site is ambiguous (two possibilities indistinguishable); should not be reported as Class I
D. 軟體錯誤D. Software bug

3. 為何分析 ubiquitination 後常難以區分 Ub 與 NEDD8？

3. Why is it hard to distinguish Ub from NEDD8 after trypsin digestion?

A. Trypsin 切後兩者都留下相同的 GG remnant，質量無差異A. Trypsin leaves identical GG remnants for both — no mass difference
B. NEDD8 不存在於人類細胞B. NEDD8 doesn't exist in human cells
C. 它們會互相反應C. They react with each other
D. 抗體不可靠D. Antibodies are unreliable
