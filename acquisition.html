LC-MS/MS 採集

DDA 與 DIA 兩大採集策略——理解它們如何決定你的蛋白質體覆蓋率與定量精準度。

DDA vs DIA — the two acquisition strategies that determine your proteome coverage and quantification precision.

概覽

從層析柱到 m/z：質譜採集的物理流程

胜肽混合物經 nanoLC 在 75 μm × 25–50 cm 的 C18 反相管柱中以梯度 ACN（5–35%，60–180 min）分離，洗脫的胜肽通過電灑離子源 (ESI) 進入質譜儀。儀器在 MS1（全掃描）記錄母離子 m/z，再選擇性對母離子進行 碰撞活化（HCD / CID）得到 MS2 碎片離子譜，後續用於資料庫比對與定量。

採集策略的核心問題是：每秒能採集多少 MS2，每個 MS2 能涵蓋多廣的 m/z 範圍？ DDA 與 DIA 給出兩種完全不同的答案。

Peptide mixtures elute from a nanoLC column (75 μm × 25–50 cm C18, ACN gradient 5–35% over 60–180 min) and enter the MS via electrospray (ESI). Each MS1 (full scan) records precursor m/z; selected precursors are then fragmented (HCD/CID) to produce MS2 spectra used for ID and quantitation.

The key acquisition question: how many MS2 scans per second, and how wide an m/z window per MS2? DDA and DIA give very different answers.

DDA vs DIA

一、DDA 與 DIA 的本質差異

🎯

DDA — Data-Dependent

「擇強者而戰」。
儀器先做 MS1 全掃描，依強度挑選 Top-N（10–30 個）最強的母離子，分別 isolation（窗 1–2 m/z）後逐一碎裂。
• 優點：MS2 純度高，鑑定可信度極佳；廣泛的 search engine 與 PTM 分析支援。
• 缺點：每次 run 只選擇性碎裂部分母離子，導致 run-to-run 不重複（missing values）。低豐度蛋白容易漏失。

"Pick the strongest." The instrument first scans MS1, then selects the top-N (10–30) most intense precursors, isolates each with a 1–2 m/z window, and fragments them.
• Pros: Clean MS2 spectra, highly reliable ID; broad search-engine and PTM support.
• Cons: Stochastic selection causes run-to-run irreproducibility (missing values). Low-abundance proteins often dropped.

🌊

DIA — Data-Independent

「全攻全守」。
儀器以預先定義的固定 m/z 窗口（例如 4–25 m/z 寬，覆蓋 400–1000 m/z 全域 25–80 個 SWATH 窗口）無差別地碎裂窗內所有母離子，產生混合 MS2。
• 優點：數據近乎完整（<5% missing），定量重現性極佳，適合大規模 cohort 研究。
• 缺點：MS2 為混合譜，需 spectral library 或 deep-learning 模型解析（DIA-NN、Spectronaut）；PTM 分析更具挑戰。

"Attack the whole thing." The instrument fragments everything within predefined m/z windows (e.g., 25–80 SWATH windows of 4–25 m/z width covering 400–1000 m/z), producing chimeric MS2 spectra.
• Pros: Near-complete data (<5% missing), excellent quantitative reproducibility, ideal for large cohorts.
• Cons: Chimeric MS2 requires spectral libraries or deep-learning models (DIA-NN, Spectronaut) to deconvolute; PTM analysis more challenging.

💡
2025 年的主流共識：探索 / PTM 研究用 DDA，定量 / 臨床 / cohort 研究用 DIA。diaPASEF（Bruker timsTOF）與 Orbitrap Astral 將兩者優點結合，已成為近兩年最強力的選項。 2025 consensus: DDA for discovery / PTM, DIA for quantitation / clinical / cohorts. diaPASEF (Bruker timsTOF) and Orbitrap Astral combine the strengths of both — the strongest options of the last two years.

三、選擇採集策略的關鍵問題

🌳 採集策略決策樹

Q1:
需要分析 PTM（特別是磷酸化）且樣品數 ≤ 12？ → 是 → DDA + TMT/SILAC + 富集。
Q2:
cohort 樣品數 > 30，需要完整 protein × sample 矩陣？ → 是 → library-free DIA（DIA-NN / Spectronaut directDIA）。
Q3:
單細胞或極微量樣品？ → 是 → diaPASEF 於 timsTOF SCP / Ultra。
Q4:
探索性研究、新 PTM 發現？ → 是 → DDA + open search（MSFragger）。
Q1:
PTM (especially phospho) and ≤12 samples? → Yes → DDA + TMT/SILAC + enrichment.
Q2:
Cohort > 30 samples, need a complete protein × sample matrix? → Yes → library-free DIA (DIA-NN / Spectronaut directDIA).
Q3:

Discovery work, novel PTMs? → Yes → DDA + open search (MSFragger).
方法設定

四、儀器方法參數示意

# Top-15 DDA on Orbitrap Exploris 480 (典型參數)
MS1 Scan:
  - Resolution:          120,000 @ m/z 200
  - Scan range:          350–1500 m/z
  - AGC target:          3e6
  - Max IT:              50 ms

MS2 Scan (Top-15):
  - Resolution:          15,000
  - Isolation window:    1.4 m/z
  - HCD NCE:             30%
  - AGC target:          1e5
  - Max IT:              22 ms
  - Charge states:       2–5+
  - Dynamic exclusion:   30 s
# Staggered-window DIA on Orbitrap Exploris 480
MS1 Scan:
  - Resolution:          60,000
  - Scan range:          400–1000 m/z
  - AGC:                 1e6,  Max IT 20 ms

DIA MS2:
  - Resolution:          30,000
  - Isolation windows:   75 × 8 m/z (overlap 4 m/z)
                         spanning 400–1000
  - HCD NCE:             30% (centered)
  - AGC:                 1e6,  Max IT 25 ms
  - Cycle time:          ~2 s  (好的 cycle 應在 2–4 s)
# diaPASEF on timsTOF Pro 2 / SCP / Ultra
PASEF Concept:
  - TIMS device fan-separates ions by 1/K0 (mobility)
  - PASEF triggers MS2 in sync with mobility separation
  - 每個 TIMS scan 內可連續取得多個 isolation window

diaPASEF schema:
  - m/z range:           400–1200 m/z
  - 1/K0 range:          0.7–1.3 Vs/cm²
  - Window width:        25 m/z × 4 mobility steps
  - Cycle time:          ~1.1 s
  - 結果：DIA + ion mobility 雙重維度，提升選擇性
