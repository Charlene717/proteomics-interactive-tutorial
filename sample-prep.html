樣品製備與消化 (Sample Preparation)

蛋白質體學成敗的關鍵第一步——細胞裂解、變性還原、酵素消化的選擇將決定下游所能偵測的蛋白質深度與品質。

The make-or-break first step — lysis, reduction/alkylation, and enzymatic digestion choices determine the proteome depth and quality achievable downstream.

概覽

為什麼樣品製備是 MS-based 蛋白質體學的命脈？

不像 scRNA-seq 可以仰賴成熟的商業化試劑套組，蛋白質體學的樣品製備變異是分析結果差異的最大來源。一份髒污或消化不完全的樣品，無論搭配多昂貴的質譜儀都救不回來。

樣品製備的核心目標有三：(1) 有效裂解細胞釋放所有蛋白質；(2) 消除干擾物（清潔劑、鹽類、脂質、核酸）以免進入質譜；(3) 完整消化蛋白質成適合 LC-MS/MS 分析的胜肽片段（通常為 7–30 個胺基酸）。

Unlike scRNA-seq, which can rely on standardized commercial kits, sample-prep variability is the single largest source of result differences in proteomics. A poorly prepared or under-digested sample cannot be rescued by even the most expensive MS instrument.

The three core goals: (1) efficient lysis to release the full proteome; (2) removal of interferents (detergents, salts, lipids, nucleic acids) that would otherwise enter the MS; (3) complete digestion into peptides (typically 7–30 amino acids) optimal for LC-MS/MS.

💡
核心原則：「Garbage in, garbage out」是蛋白質體學的金句。在比較不同樣品時，所有樣品必須使用完全相同的製備流程，避免技術變異被誤判為生物學差異。 Core principle: "Garbage in, garbage out" applies forcefully here. Across compared samples, use an identical prep protocol to avoid technical variation masquerading as biology.
核心流程

一、典型 Bottom-up 樣品製備流程

細胞裂解

使用變性 detergent（SDS 4–5%、SDC、Urea 8 M、Guanidine HCl 6 M）破壞細胞膜並使蛋白質完全變性，避免後續分析中重新摺疊。組織樣品需先機械破碎。

Use denaturing detergents (4–5% SDS, SDC, 8 M urea, 6 M guanidine HCl) to disrupt membranes and fully denature proteins, preventing refolding. Tissues require mechanical disruption first.

還原與烷化

用 DTT（10–20 mM, 56°C）或 TCEP 還原雙硫鍵，再用 IAA（碘乙醯胺，20–55 mM）烷化半胱胺酸，防止重新形成雙硫鍵造成消化不完全。需在暗處進行避免 IAA 過度反應。

Reduce disulfide bonds with DTT (10–20 mM at 56°C) or TCEP, then alkylate cysteines with iodoacetamide (IAA, 20–55 mM) to prevent re-oxidation that would impair digestion. Perform IAA step in darkness to avoid overalkylation.

蛋白質純化 / 清潔劑移除

關鍵步驟！SDS / Urea 等變性劑會抑制酵素活性與離子化效率。使用 FASP、SP3、S-Trap 或 In-solution + acetone precipitation 將蛋白質保留並去除干擾物。

A critical step. SDS/urea inhibit enzymes and ionization. Use FASP, SP3, S-Trap, or in-solution + acetone precipitation to retain proteins while removing interferents.

酵素消化

最常用 胰蛋白酶 (Trypsin)，在 K/R 後切割（除非後面是 P）。Enzyme:Protein 比例約 1:50，37°C 過夜或 4–8 小時。Lys-C + Trypsin 雙重消化可提升完整度。

Most common is trypsin, cleaving C-terminal to K/R (except before P). Enzyme:protein ≈ 1:50, 37°C overnight or 4–8 h. Lys-C + trypsin tandem digestion improves completeness.

脫鹽

使用 C18 SPE（Stage-tip、cartridge）移除鹽類與消化產物中的小分子，並濃縮樣品。送上機前必須完成。

Use C18 SPE (Stage-tips, cartridges) to remove salts and small molecules and concentrate peptides. Mandatory before MS injection.

方法比較

二、四大主流蛋白質純化／消化方法

🧴

In-solution

最簡單流程，但要求 detergent 兼容（如低濃度尿素）。使用 acetone/TCA 沉澱去除清潔劑。
• 優點：無需特殊耗材，便宜
• 缺點：清潔劑兼容性受限；可重複性較低

Simplest workflow but limited to MS-compatible buffers (e.g., low urea). Acetone/TCA precipitation can remove detergents.
• Pro: No special consumables, cheap
• Con: Limited detergent tolerance; lower reproducibility

🪣

FASP (Filter-Aided)

使用 10/30 kDa MWCO 離心過濾管：蛋白質保留在膜上，SDS 等小分子被沖洗掉。原位消化。
• 優點：能處理高濃度 SDS；流程經典
• 缺點：步驟多、時間長（4–6 hr）；濾膜可能造成蛋白質損失

10/30 kDa MWCO spin filters retain proteins while SDS is washed away; on-filter digestion follows.
• Pro: Tolerates high SDS; classic protocol
• Con: Many steps, slow (4–6 hr); filter losses possible

🧲

SP3 (磁珠法)

Single-Pot Solid-Phase-enhanced Sample Preparation：利用 carboxylate 修飾磁珠在乙醇/ACN 環境中吸附蛋白質，洗去干擾物後消化。
• 優點：快速（1–2 hr）；適合 low-input；近年最受歡迎
• 缺點：磁珠質量與比例需優化

Single-Pot Solid-Phase-enhanced Sample Preparation: carboxylate-modified magnetic beads capture proteins in ethanol/ACN, contaminants are washed off, then digestion proceeds.
• Pro: Fast (1–2 hr); excellent for low input; the most popular method in recent years
• Con: Bead quality and ratio require optimization

🏔️

S-Trap (Suspension Trap)

蛋白質在酸性 + 甲醇中沉澱後被困在 trap column 的疏鬆基質中，原位高溫消化。
• 優點：高 SDS（≤5%）容忍；快速（30–90 min）；可重複性佳
• 缺點：商用 column 較貴；超低量（<1 µg）覆蓋率受限

Proteins precipitate in acid+methanol, get trapped in a porous matrix, then digested in situ at elevated temperature.
• Pro: Tolerates ≤5% SDS; fast (30–90 min); excellent reproducibility
• Con: Proprietary columns are pricey; coverage limited for ultra-low input (<1 µg)

互動模擬

消化效率模擬器

調整 酵素比例、消化時間與是否使用 Lys-C 預消化，觀察 fully-cleaved（完整消化）、missed cleavage（漏切）、與超短胜肽（<7 aa）的比例變化。理想狀態為 missed cleavage < 25%。

Adjust enzyme ratio, digestion time, and whether you use Lys-C pre-digestion. Watch how fully-cleaved, missed-cleavage, and over-short (<7 aa) peptide fractions change. Ideal: missed cleavage < 25%.

決策引導

三、選擇樣品製備方法的決策流程

🌳 樣品製備決策樹

Q1:
樣品起始量 < 10 µg（如組織針穿、單細胞 group）？ → 是 → 首選 SP3；可選 iST-NHS 或 nanoPOTS（單細胞）。
Q2:
樣品含大量膜蛋白或需用高濃度 SDS？ → 是 → 首選 S-Trap（5% SDS）；次選 FASP。
Q3:
需要極高通量（96-well, 自動化）？ → 是 → SP3 自動化平台（Kingfisher、Bravo）。
Q4:
研究 PTM（特別是磷酸化）需要超大蛋白量？ → 是 → In-solution + acetone precipitation（mg 級起始量更穩定）。
Q1:
Starting input < 10 µg (needle biopsy, sorted cells)? → Yes → SP3 first; iST-NHS or nanoPOTS (single-cell) as alternatives.
Q2:
Membrane-rich sample or needs high-% SDS? → Yes → S-Trap (handles 5% SDS); FASP as a backup.
Q3:
High-throughput needed (96-well, automation)? → Yes → Automated SP3 (Kingfisher, Bravo).
Q4:
PTM (especially phospho) study with abundant input? → Yes → In-solution + acetone precipitation (more stable at mg-scale).

⚠️
專家建議：
① 務必驗證消化效率——分析 missed cleavage rate（理想 < 25%）。
② 多樣品比較時，同一批一起做，避免日內變異。
③ 含血漿樣品先做 top-12 蛋白移除（如 Albumin、IgG），否則高豐度蛋白會壓制其他訊號。
④ 含 RNA 的細胞核樣品要先加 Benzonase 降解核酸，避免膠狀沉澱。
Expert tips:
① Always verify digestion efficiency via missed-cleavage rate (ideally < 25%).
② For multi-sample comparisons, prep all samples in the same batch to avoid day-to-day drift.
③ Plasma samples need top-12 depletion (albumin, IgG, etc.) — high-abundance proteins suppress the rest.
④ Nuclear/RNA-rich samples need Benzonase to digest nucleic acids and avoid gel-like clumps.
實作範例

SP3 與 S-Trap 操作示範

# SP3 sample prep (Hughes et al. 2019, Nat Protoc)
# 起始: 10–100 µg protein in 5% SDS + 100 mM TEAB

STEP 1 Reduce: 10 mM DTT, 95°C, 10 min
STEP 2 Alkylate: 40 mM IAA, RT, 30 min dark
STEP 3 Bind to beads:
    - Mix 1:1 hydrophilic + hydrophobic SP3 beads (5 µL of 50 µg/µL each)
    - Add ethanol to 50% final
    - Incubate RT, 5 min, gentle mixing
STEP 4 Wash on magnet:
    - 3× 80% ethanol (500 µL each)
    - 1× 100% acetonitrile
STEP 5 Digest:
    - Resuspend beads in 100 mM TEAB
    - Add trypsin (1:50 enzyme:protein)
    - Sonicate briefly, incubate 37°C overnight
STEP 6 Elute:
    - Collect supernatant
    - Wash beads with 2% DMSO, combine
    - Acidify with TFA to pH < 2
STEP 7 Desalt with C18 Stage-tips
# S-Trap micro/mini column protocol
# 起始: 10–300 µg protein in 5% SDS + 50 mM TEAB

STEP 1 Reduce + alkylate (as SP3)
STEP 2 Acidify:
    - Add 12% phosphoric acid (1:10) → final 1.2%
    - Verify yellow color (Bromophenol Blue indicator)
STEP 3 Precipitate + load:
    - Add 6× volume of S-Trap binding buffer
      (90% methanol + 100 mM TEAB)
    - Load onto S-Trap column, spin 4000 g
STEP 4 Wash 3× with binding buffer
STEP 5 Digest in-column:
    - Add trypsin in 50 mM TEAB (1:25)
    - Incubate 47°C, 1–2 hr (or 37°C overnight)
STEP 6 Elute peptides:
    - 50 mM TEAB → 0.2% formic acid → 50% ACN/0.2% FA
    - Combine all eluates
STEP 7 Dry in SpeedVac, resuspend, desalt
import numpy as np
from scipy.interpolate import interp1d

# BCA assay: BSA standards (mg/mL) and A562 readings
bsa = np.array([0, 0.125, 0.25, 0.5, 1.0, 1.5, 2.0])
abs562 = np.array([0.04, 0.18, 0.32, 0.58, 1.05, 1.42, 1.78])

# Fit standard curve (linear range)
slope, intercept = np.polyfit(bsa[1:5], abs562[1:5], 1)

# Unknown samples (already adjusted for dilution)
unknowns = {"S1": 0.62, "S2": 0.88, "S3": 0.41}
for name, A in unknowns.items():
    conc = (A - intercept) / slope
    print(f"{name}: {conc:.2f} mg/mL")
# BCA quantification in R
bsa <- c(0, 0.125, 0.25, 0.5, 1.0, 1.5, 2.0)
abs562 <- c(0.04, 0.18, 0.32, 0.58, 1.05, 1.42, 1.78)

# Linear fit on quantitative range
fit <- lm(abs562[2:5] ~ bsa[2:5])
summary(fit)$r.squared

# Apply to unknowns
unknowns <- c(S1 = 0.62, S2 = 0.88, S3 = 0.41)
conc <- (unknowns - coef(fit)[1]) / coef(fit)[2]
print(round(conc, 2))
