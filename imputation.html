STEP 7 / 9

缺失值處理 (Missing Value Imputation)

分辨 MNAR 與 MAR 兩種缺失機制——選錯方法會引入系統性偏差。

Distinguish MNAR vs MAR — choosing the wrong imputer introduces systematic bias.

概念

為何蛋白質體學的缺失值處理比想像中複雜？

蛋白質體學資料的缺失率通常 10–40%（DDA 較高，DIA 較低）。核心問題是：缺失值不是「隨機」的——低豐度蛋白更容易因偵測極限（LOD）而缺失。這意味著「補 0」、「補平均值」、或「直接刪除」都會引入嚴重偏差。

正確做法：先診斷缺失機制，再選擇對應的 imputation 演算法。

Proteomics data have 10–40% missing values (higher in DDA, lower in DIA). The key issue: missing values are NOT random — low-abundance proteins are more likely to drop below the detection limit (LOD). This means "replace with 0", "replace with mean", or "drop the row" all introduce serious bias.

The right approach: diagnose the missingness mechanism first, then pick the matching imputer.

兩大機制

一、MNAR vs MAR 兩種缺失機制

📉

MNAR — Missing Not At Random

「低於偵測極限」造成的缺失：蛋白質本來就很低甚至為 0，所以才測不到。
• 特徵：同一蛋白在某個 condition 全部缺失，另一 condition 全部偵測到（左尾、左截尾）。
• 適用 imputation：從 low intensity（如 detected 分布的 1% 分位）抽樣，例如 QRILC、MinDet、MinProb。
• 不要用 KNN / RF — 它們會把低值「補成接近群體平均」，扭曲真實的「低豐度」訊號。

Caused by signals below detection limit: the protein is genuinely low or absent.
• Pattern: Protein missing entirely in one condition, detected in another (left-tailed, left-censored).
• Appropriate imputers: Sample from the low end (e.g., 1st percentile of observed) — QRILC, MinDet, MinProb.
• Don't use KNN / RF — they pull low values toward the population mean, distorting the genuine "low signal".

🎲

MAR — Missing At Random

「偶然性測不到」：蛋白質本來足以偵測，但因 DDA 的隨機選擇、其他干擾、或單次失敗而缺失。
• 特徵：缺失隨機分布在 conditions 與 replicates 之間。
• 適用 imputation：利用其他樣品的觀測值「猜測」缺失值，例如 KNN、Random Forest (missForest)、SVD/BPCA、LLS。

The protein is detectable but missed by chance — DDA stochastic selection, interference, or single-run failure.
• Pattern: Missingness randomly distributed across conditions/replicates.
• Appropriate imputers: Use observed values from other samples to "guess" — KNN, Random Forest (missForest), SVD/BPCA, LLS.

💡
實務做法 (DEP / MSstats 推薦)：使用「Mixed imputation」——先判斷每個蛋白的缺失模式，MNAR 蛋白用 left-censored 方法，MAR 蛋白用 KNN/RF 方法。這就是當前 2024 benchmark 中表現最佳的策略。 Practical approach (DEP / MSstats): Use "Mixed imputation" — diagnose each protein's missingness pattern; impute MNAR proteins with left-censored methods, MAR proteins with KNN/RF. This is the top-performing strategy in 2024 benchmarks.

# missingness 與差異表達，比後處理 imputation 更穩健
import pandas as pd
import numpy as np
from sklearn.impute import KNNImputer

mat = pd.read_csv("protein_log2.csv", index_col=0)

# --- MNAR (QRILC-like): sample from left tail ---
def impute_mnar(df, q=0.01, scale=0.3):
    out = df.copy()
    for col in df.columns:
        mu = df[col].quantile(q)
        sd = df[col].std() * scale
        mask = df[col].isna()
        out.loc[mask, col] = np.random.normal(mu, sd, mask.sum())
    return out

# --- MAR (KNN) ---
imp = KNNImputer(n_neighbors=10, weights="distance")
mat_knn = pd.DataFrame(imp.fit_transform(mat),
                       index=mat.index, columns=mat.columns)

# --- Mixed: diagnose per protein ---
mnar_proteins = mat.index[mat.isna().sum(axis=1) > 0.4 * mat.shape[1]]
mat_mix = mat_knn.copy()
mat_mix.loc[mnar_proteins] = impute_mnar(mat.loc[mnar_proteins])

⚠️
專家警告：① 即使做 imputation，仍應在報告中註明使用的方法與比例。② 若蛋白只在「疾病組偵測到」而完全在「健康組缺失」，這可能是真實生物差異——不要強行用 KNN 把它拉成中等值。③ MSstats 的 model-based imputation 內建處理 missingness 的 mixed model，通常優於後處理 imputation。
Expert warnings: ① Always report the method and the fraction of imputed values. ② A protein detected only in disease and absent in healthy may reflect true biology — don't let KNN flatten it. ③ MSstats's model-based imputation handles missingness within its mixed model, typically outperforming post-hoc imputation.
