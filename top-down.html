Top-down 完整蛋白質分析

不消化、直接分析完整蛋白——讓多重 PTM 與 splicing isoforms 一次顯現。

Skip digestion, analyze intact proteins — reveal co-occurring PTMs and splicing isoforms in a single spectrum.

概念

一、為什麼 Top-down 不可取代？

Bottom-up 把蛋白切碎成 peptide，所以雖然可以鑑定到「序列上哪個位點有 PTM」，但失去了「同一條蛋白上多個 PTM 是否共同存在」的資訊。例如：histone H3 上 K9me3 與 K27ac 是在同一分子上同時發生嗎？bottom-up 無法回答。

Top-down 直接分析整段蛋白，可同時辨識：(1) 全部 PTM 種類與位置；(2) PTM 之間的共現/互斥關係；(3) splicing isoform；(4) 序列變異（如 SAAV）。這就是「proteoform」（單一分子層級的蛋白形式）概念的核心。

Bottom-up shreds proteins into peptides — although you can map "which residue has the PTM", you lose the information about whether multiple PTMs co-occur on the same molecule. For example: are H3 K9me3 and K27ac on the same H3 protein at the same time? Bottom-up can't answer this.

Top-down analyzes the full protein, simultaneously revealing: (1) all PTM types and positions; (2) co-occurrence / mutual exclusion of PTMs; (3) splicing isoforms; (4) sequence variants (SAAVs). This is the heart of the "proteoform" concept — protein forms at the single-molecule level.

💡
2024 Nature Reviews Methods Primers 將 top-down 列為「unambiguous proteoform identification」的唯一方法。應用領域包括: histone PTM crosstalk、抗體 (mAb) charge variant、心肌肌鈣蛋白磷酸化 patterns、神經退化疾病蛋白聚集。 The 2024 Nature Reviews Methods Primer lists top-down as the only method for "unambiguous proteoform identification". Applications: histone PTM crosstalk, mAb charge variants, cardiac troponin phospho patterns, neurodegenerative protein aggregates.
工作流程

二、Top-down 工作流程

蛋白質提取與還原

使用溫和裂解（avoid SDS），保留蛋白完整折疊；TCEP 還原雙硫鍵。

Gentle lysis (avoid SDS) preserves protein integrity; TCEP reduces disulfides.

蛋白分離

RPLC、Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC)、Capillary IEF (CIEF) 或 CZE 等分離法；目標縮小複雜度。

RPLC, SEC, capillary IEF (CIEF), or CZE — narrow complexity prior to MS.

完整蛋白質譜 (MS1)

使用高解析度儀器（Orbitrap UHMR、FT-ICR），記錄完整蛋白的 multi-charge 包絡，deconvolution 後得到單一 monoisotopic mass。

Use high-res instruments (Orbitrap UHMR, FT-ICR). Record multi-charge envelopes of intact proteins, then deconvolute to a single monoisotopic mass.

碎裂與 MS2

用 ETD/UVPD/ECD 避免破壞 PTM；產生 c/z 系列碎片。連續碎片覆蓋整條序列即可推定 PTM 位置。

Use ETD/UVPD/ECD to preserve PTMs; generate c/z ion series. Continuous fragment coverage along the sequence pinpoints PTM positions.

軟體分析

主流：TopPIC、ProSight Lite/PD、MASH Explorer、Informed-Proteomics。Proteoform 命名遵循 PSI 標準。

Major tools: TopPIC, ProSight Lite/PD, MASH Explorer, Informed-Proteomics. Proteoform naming follows PSI standards.

互動模擬

Proteoform 共現模擬

Bottom-up 顯示「PTM A 與 PTM B 都存在於某 protein」，但無法判斷它們是「共存於同一分子」（cis）或「在不同分子」（trans）。Top-down 直接區分。

Bottom-up shows "both PTM A and B exist on this protein" but can't tell if they're on the same molecule (cis) or different molecules (trans). Top-down distinguishes these directly.

範例

三、軟體與案例

🛠️ TopPIC

開源工具，可在 PNNL 平台執行。輸入 deconvoluted MS1+MS2，輸出 proteoform identifications 與 site-localization probability。

Open-source pipeline on the PNNL platform. Input: deconvoluted MS1+MS2. Output: proteoform IDs and site-localization probabilities.

🔬 ProSight PD (Thermo)

商用節點，與 Proteome Discoverer 整合。提供「Subsequence search」、「Δ mass」、與圖形化 proteoform browser，是 mAb characterization 的工業標準。

Commercial node integrated with Proteome Discoverer. Subsequence search, Δ-mass, and graphical proteoform browser — industry standard for mAb characterization.

📚 Histone H3 PTM crosstalk

2024 Cell 研究使用 top-down 證明 H3K27ac 與 H3K9me3 「mutual exclusive」——同一 H3 分子不會同時 carry 兩者。這推翻了 bottom-up 推測的「共存」假設。

A 2024 Cell paper used top-down to prove H3K27ac and H3K9me3 are mutually exclusive on the same H3 molecule — overturning a "co-existence" hypothesis inferred from bottom-up.

💊 mAb charge variants

單株抗體製造常出現 N/C 端裁切、Gln→Pyro-Glu、糖基化異質性。Top-down 可在生產線快速辨識並 quantify 各 charge variant，已成 QC 標準。

mAb manufacturing yields N/C-terminal clipping, Gln→pyro-Glu, and glycosylation heterogeneity. Top-down rapidly identifies and quantifies charge variants in production — now a QC standard.

2. 為何 top-down 偏好 ETD/UVPD 而非 HCD？

2. Why does top-down prefer ETD/UVPD over HCD?

A. ETD/UVPD 更便宜A. Cheaper
B. ETD/UVPD 速度更快B. Faster
C. ETD/UVPD 不破壞 labile PTMs（如磷酸化、糖基化）；能在完整大蛋白上覆蓋更完整的序列C. ETD/UVPD preserves labile PTMs (phospho, glyco) and gives more complete sequence coverage on large proteins
D. HCD 對 top-down 無效D. HCD doesn't work at all
